## 描述（4000字符内）

Can you reach 2048 before time runs out?

2048 Merge is the addictive number puzzle that challenges your brain AND your speed. Swipe, merge, and race the clock in this fresh take on the classic 2048 formula.

HOW TO PLAY
Swipe in any direction to slide all tiles. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one — doubling their value. Your goal? Hit the target number before time runs out!

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT
⏱ TIME PRESSURE — Regular 2048 is relaxing. Our version is thrilling. Every second counts!
🎯 LEVEL PROGRESSION — Start at 256, work your way up to the legendary 4096
🧠 PURE SKILL — No luck involved. Just strategy, speed, and smarts
📵 PLAY OFFLINE — No Wi-Fi needed. Perfect for planes, commutes, and waiting rooms
🎨 SATISFYING ANIMATIONS — Every merge feels amazing with our smooth tile effects
